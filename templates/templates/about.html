About The Trompo King

Bringing Authentic Mexican Cuisine to Your Community

Our Story

The Trompo King was founded with a simple mission: to bring authentic Mexican Al Pastor tacos and traditional cuisine to the community. Starting from a passion for authentic Mexican flavors, we've grown to 3 thriving locations while maintaining our commitment to quality and authenticity.

Every recipe is carefully crafted using traditional methods passed down through generations, ensuring that every bite transports you to the streets of Mexico City. Our chefs are trained in authentic Mexican culinary arts and use only the finest, freshest ingredients.

Our Mission

To provide an authentic Mexican dining experience that celebrates traditional recipes, honors culinary heritage, and builds community through exceptional food and warm hospitality.

Core Values:

  • Authenticity: We never compromise on traditional recipes and cooking methods
  • Quality: Premium ingredients sourced for superior taste and nutrition
  • Community: Creating gathering places where people can enjoy great food together
  • Excellence: Striving for perfection in every dish we serve
  • Passion: Cooking with love and dedication to our craft

Our Specialty: Al Pastor Tacos

Al Pastor is a traditional Mexican cooking technique that dates back centuries. The iconic spinning vertical rotisserie, called a "trompo," slowly roasts marinated meat infused with spices, pineapple, and aromatic herbs. This method creates incredibly tender, flavorful meat that's the foundation of our signature tacos.

Our Al Pastor tacos feature:

  • Slow-roasted marinated pork cooked to perfection
  • Fresh pineapple for authentic flavor and sweetness
  • Homemade soft corn tortillas
  • Crispy onion and fresh cilantro
  • Our signature house salsa
